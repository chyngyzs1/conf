Abstract Submission Format

Abstracts

  • Abstracts can be submitted only in English.
  • The maximal length of the abstract is 1(one) page.
  • The abstract should be prepared in LaTex (as a LaTeX-file) and carefully checked for misprints. It must not include automatic numbering and automatic links to formulas, theorems, article, etc.
  • Please do not use your own macros and do not include pictures in the abstract.
  • Please use the following templates when preparing your abstract: Abstract_Template .
  • Please do not edit the style file MADEA9.sty.
  • The TeX- and PDF-files of the abstract should be sent to madea2021@manas.edu.kg.
  • The deadline for abstract submission is April 30, 2021. Submission of abstracts has been extended until May 10, 2021. .
  • The Organizing Committee reserves the right to reject submitted abstracts that do not match the topics of the conference or do not meet the requirements.
