History

This is the ninth Kyrgyz-Turkish-Ukrainian international scientific conference in the field of mathematical analysis, differential equations and their applications.

  • The first conference was held in August 26-30, 2003 in National Juriy Fedkovich University of Chernivtsi (Chernivtsi, Ukraine)
  • The second was held in September 07-11, 2004 in Mersin University (Mersin, Turkey)
  • The third was held in September 18-23, 2006 in Uzhgorod National University (Uzhgorod, Ukraine)
  • The fourth was held in September 12-15, 2008 at Eastern Mediterranean University (Famagusta, North Cyprus).
  • The fifth was held in September 15-20, 2010 at Sunny Beach, Bulgaria.
  • The sixth was held in September 04-09, 2012 in Mersin University (Mersin, Turkey)
  • The seventh was held in September 08-13, 2015 in Institute of Mathematics and Mechanics NAS of Azerbaijan (Baku, Azerbaijan)
  • The eighth was held in June 17-23, 2018 at the Kyrgyz-Turkish University "Manas" (Bishkek, Kyrgyz Republic).
